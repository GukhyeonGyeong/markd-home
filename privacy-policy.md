# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 29, 2026**

Eyaa Moment provides the MarkD app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of information when you use our app.

## Your Documents Stay on Your Device

MarkD does not collect any personally identifiable information. All documents you save, import, or share into MarkD are stored locally on your device using iOS App Group storage, shared only between the MarkD app and its Share Extension. **Document content never leaves your device.**

## Share Extension

When you use the Share Extension to save content from other apps, that content is saved directly to your device. It is not sent to any external server or third party.

## Third-Party Services for Crash Reporting and Analytics

MarkD uses the following Google Firebase SDKs to improve app stability and quality:

- **Firebase Crashlytics** — collects anonymous crash reports (stack traces, device model, OS version) when the app crashes, so we can identify and fix bugs.
- **Firebase Analytics** — collects anonymous, aggregate usage data (which screens are viewed, which features are used) to understand how people use the app and improve it.

**What is collected:**

| Data | Purpose | Linked to your identity | Used for tracking |
|---|---|---|---|
| Crash data (stack traces, device model, OS version) | Bug fixing | No | No |
| Other diagnostic data | Performance monitoring | No | No |
| Product interaction (screen views, feature usage) | Analytics | No | No |
| Device ID (anonymous) | Analytics, app functionality | No | No |

**What is NOT collected:**

- The contents of your documents
- File names, folder names, tag names
- Search queries
- Personally identifiable information (no name, email, phone number, or account)
- Location, contacts, photos, or any device sensors
- Advertising identifier (IDFA)

We do not use this data to track you across other companies' apps or websites. We do not sell or share this data with anyone.

For Google Firebase's own privacy practices, see: <https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy>

## Children's Privacy

MarkD does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.
